Kitchen Cost FAQ

Common questions about modular kitchen prices in Bangalore. Answered honestly.

How much does a modular kitchen cost in Bangalore?

At Elvenwood, a straight modular kitchen starts from ₹1.08 Lakh, L-shaped from ₹1.96 Lakh, and U-shaped from ₹2.55 Lakh. All prices are factory-direct with no middleman markup. Prices include cabinets, hardware, finish, and installation.

What is the cost per sq ft for a modular kitchen?

It depends on the finish you choose. Laminate is ₹1,176/sq ft, Acrylic ₹1,764/sq ft, PU (Polyurethane) ₹2,200/sq ft, and Lacquer/Veneer ₹2,940/sq ft. All include BWP plywood carcass and Hettich soft-close hardware.

Is countertop included in the kitchen price?

Countertop, chimney, hob, and sink are quoted separately. The kitchen price includes upper and lower cabinets, Hettich soft-close hardware, BWP plywood carcass, your chosen finish, skirting, installation, and a 10-year warranty.

How much does an L-shaped kitchen cost?

An L-shaped modular kitchen starts from ₹1.96 Lakh with laminate finish, and ₹2.50 Lakh or more for acrylic or PU finishes. The final cost depends on size (8-12 ft total), accessories, and the finish you choose.

Which is cheaper — modular kitchen or carpenter?

A modular kitchen is 20-30% more upfront but lasts 15+ years compared to 5-7 years for carpenter work. You get better finish quality, precision hardware, a proper warranty, and higher resale value. Factory-built kitchens also deliver consistent quality that carpenter work cannot match.

How long to build a modular kitchen?

28-45 days from design approval to handover. Our 17,400 sq ft factory with Italian Biesse CNC machines ensures precise, fast production. Installation typically takes 1-2 days on site.

Do you offer EMI for modular kitchens?

Yes. We offer flexible EMI options. Free consultation and 3D design visualization are included before any payment commitment. Contact us on WhatsApp for EMI plan details.

Can I visit the factory to see kitchens being made?

Yes! Our 17,400 sq ft factory and Experience Centre is in Bommasandra Industrial Area, Bangalore. Visit Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 9 PM. See the machines, touch the materials, and watch your kitchen being built.

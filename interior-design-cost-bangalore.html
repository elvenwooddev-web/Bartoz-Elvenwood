Pricing FAQs

Common questions about interior design costs in Bangalore.

How much does 2BHK interior design cost in Bangalore?

2BHK interior design starts from ₹3.33 Lakh at our Essential tier. Includes modular kitchen, 2 wardrobes, TV unit, and shoe rack. Factory-direct pricing with 45-day delivery and 10-year warranty.

How much does 3BHK interior design cost in Bangalore?

3BHK interior design starts from ₹4.70 Lakh at our Essential tier. Full home interior with kitchen, 3 wardrobes, TV unit, study area, and crockery unit. All rooms designed cohesively.

What is the cost per sq ft for interior design in Bangalore?

Essential tier: ₹1,176/sq ft. Classic tier: ₹1,764/sq ft. Premium tier: ₹2,940/sq ft. Prices include materials, hardware, and installation.

What's included in 2BHK interior package?

Essential: Modular kitchen, 2 wardrobes, TV unit, shoe rack. Classic adds false ceiling and painting. Premium includes electrical, plumbing, flooring, wallpaper, and lighting design.

Is painting included in interior design cost?

In Classic and Premium tiers, yes. The Essential tier covers modular furniture only — kitchen, wardrobes, TV units, and storage.

Do you offer EMI?

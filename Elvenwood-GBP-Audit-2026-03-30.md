## Review Generation Strategy

### The System

Elvenwood completes approximately 8-10 projects per month. With a systematic approach, each project should generate 1-2 reviews, targeting 8-12 new reviews per month.

### Step 1: Create the Direct Review Link

1. Go to Google Business Profile Manager
2. Click "Get more reviews" or "Share review form"
3. Copy the short URL
4. Create a memorable short link (e.g., using Bitly): `bit.ly/elvenwood-review`

### Step 2: The "Moment of Delight" Ask

**When to ask:** On handover day, after the customer sees their completed kitchen/wardrobe/home for the first time and expresses delight.

**The script (in person):**
> "We're so glad you love how it turned out! Would you mind sharing your experience on Google? It really helps other families in [their area] find us. I can WhatsApp you a direct link right now — it takes about 30 seconds."

**Then immediately send via WhatsApp:**
```
Hi [Name]! Thank you for trusting Elvenwood with your [kitchen/home/wardrobe]!

If you have a moment, we'd really appreciate a Google review — it helps
other families find us.

Here's a direct link (takes 30 seconds): [review link]

Thank you for being part of the Elvenwood family!
```

### Step 3: Follow-Up Sequence

| Timing | Channel | Message |
|--------|---------|---------|
| Handover day | In-person ask + WhatsApp link | Primary ask |
| Day 3 | WhatsApp | "Hope you're enjoying the new [kitchen]! If you haven't had a chance, here's the review link: [link]" |
| Day 7 | WhatsApp (final) | "Hi [Name], just a gentle reminder — your review would mean the world to us: [link]" |

### Step 4: Encouraging Keyword-Rich Reviews

You cannot tell customers what to write, but you can shape the conversation:

- **Instead of:** "Can you leave us a review?"
- **Say:** "How did the modular kitchen turn out? Would you mind sharing that on Google?"

The customer's natural response will include "modular kitchen" — creating organic keyword mentions.

**Other conversation starters:**
- "How's the wardrobe working out for the family?"
- "Was the 45-day delivery what you expected?"
- "What did you think of the factory visit?"

### Step 5: Review Response Protocol

| Review Type | Response Time | Key Elements |
|-------------|--------------|--------------|
| 5-star | Within 24 hours | Use name, reference specific project/service, mention location, warm close |
| 4-star | Within 24 hours | Thank them, acknowledge any suggestion, reference project details |
| 3-star or below | Within 12 hours | Acknowledge issue, don't be defensive, take offline, offer resolution |
| Question in review | Within 12 hours | Answer the question helpfully, include phone number for follow-up |

### Step 6: Tracking

Track monthly in a simple spreadsheet:
